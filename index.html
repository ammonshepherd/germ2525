This course broadens and deepens your language proficiency in a project-oriented way. You will learn to develop a German-language website, print and discuss iconic representations of German and US-American culture using 3D printers, and create projects and games using simple electronic circuits. Throughout this course, we approach language learning in an inventive, playful way that focuses on creating and making. Requirements include regular attendance and participation, short team-based presentations, and a web-based learning portfolio documenting your progress.

The successful completion of GERM 2525 fulfills the language requirement of the College of Arts and Sciences. Prerequisites: GERM 2010 or corresponding qualification).
